Frequently Asked Questions

Is Cookie Manager safe to use?

Yes, Cookie Manager operates locally and never sends your cookie data to external servers. All operations happen directly in your browser.

How do I import cookies?

Simply click the "Import" button, select your cookie file, and follow the on-screen instructions.

Which browsers are supported?

Cookie Manager works with Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and other Chromium-based browsers.
